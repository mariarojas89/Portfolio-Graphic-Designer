Keep your visual content in motion!

Beyond being attention-grabbing, motion graphics affect our emotions, by immediately empathize with the emotions of the experiences we see dislayed on a screen. As an animator my purpose is to comunicate throughout a powerfull visual storytelling.

Animation adds energy to the story you want to tell

Motion graphics allow for a rich story to be carried through multiple channels of communication, but they demand a delicate balance of interwoven components. Music swells and ebbs with key turning points. Artwork gives a face to abstract language. Propelling the narrative forward. Sound design draws attention to important messaging. And the voiceover adds a human connection. All must work together harmoniously throughout the duration of the motion graphic to keep the audience engaged.
